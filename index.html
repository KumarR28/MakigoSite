  • About Us

    Who says great sushi has to be expensive sushi?

    Who says you can't get great sushi fast? Why can't you ever just have exactly what you want? Well, here at maki go! we believe you can have great sushi, quickly, casually, and just the way you like it. From start to finish, the options, endless... the flavors, fresh, healthy, and delicious.

    Back Home

    Born and raised in Columbus, this town has rightfully earned its stripes as a true foodie town. But after spending sometime in California, Columbus seemed to be missing just one thing. Sushi that anyone, from newbie to sushi connoisseur, could go to enjoy that was affordable, fast, yet maintain the essence of old traditional preparations of the fish and rice. So, Maki Go! was created to being a whole new experience to the sushi world. Where you, our guest, are essentially the chef, and we're just the hands that create it for you.
